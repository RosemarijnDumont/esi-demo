# Training Plan: Optimized Financial Export Process

This document outlines the training plan for the finance team on the newly optimized financial export process and tools.

## 1. Training Objectives

Upon completion of this training, participants will be able to:

*   Understand the reasons for the process optimization and its benefits.
*   Navigate the export portal and initiate the quarterly financial audit export.
*   Monitor the export progress and interpret various status messages.
*   Effectively troubleshoot common issues encountered during the export process.
*   Know when and how to escalate issues to the IT support team.

## 2. Target Audience

*   All members of the Finance Department involved in financial audits and data extraction.
